The studio

One city, one palette, one obsession with the frame.

Photo Studio BKK is a Bangkok-based practice devoted entirely to black-and-white image-making. We strip away colour to leave what matters: light, gesture, and presence.

From our daylight studio in the heart of the city, we work with individuals, agencies, and brands who want imagery that feels timeless — never trend-bound.
